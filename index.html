Some of the Things I Do

There are many different routes to go as a graphic designer. Here are some of my specialties!

Illustration

Illustration brings imagination to life by putting the pencil or cursor to the page. I create custom illustrations both digitally and by hand, sometimes using a combination of both.

Branding

A strong brand creates an advantage by increasing not only recognition, but trust in a product or company. I love creating branding plans that show consistency across multiple types of media.

Photography

Some say a picture is worth a thousand words. By incorporating original photography in my work, I am able to create unique designs you will not find anywhere else.

About Me!

Stick around and get to know me!

I am a Miami based graphic designer who specializes in branding, logo design, and illustration. I am currently attending Miami International University of Art and Design where I am majoring in Graphic and Web Design. I am a Miami native with multicultural roots, so I know what appeals to different demographics. This together with my creativity, experience, and can do attitude make me an excellent choice to hire for your next project! Aside from the previously mentioned skills, I have experience with package design, photography, image manipulation, digital airbrushing. I also am extremely familiar with design principles, HTML5 and CSS3 coding, color theory, and have a strong background in typography.
